Kale Salad

Description

Fatty, spicy, invigorating, and fresh, this dark green kale salad with avocado is a quick and healthy addition to any main meal, or is a perfect lunch on its own!

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches kale, stemmed and finely chopped
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 ripe avocados, mashed
  • 1 pint grape tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 bunch green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Combine kale and lemon juice in a bowl. Vigorously massage kale by squeezing handfuls for about 1 minute. Add avocados, tomatoes, and green onions.
  2. Mix together olive oil, garlic, salt, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl. Pour dressing over salad and mix well. Serve immediately.
