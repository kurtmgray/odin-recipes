Apple Pie Cocktail

Description

All the flavors of fall in a glass! Fireball® whiskey, vanilla vodka, and sparkling apple cider make this a very refreshing drink. You can add sliced apples as a garnish or pomegranate seeds for a festive look! Perfect for Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce vanilla-flavored vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce cinnamon whiskey (such as Fireball®)
  • ¼ fluid ounce lemon juice
  • ice cubes
  • ½ cup chilled sparkling apple cider
  • 1 dash cardamom bitters

Steps

  1. Combine vodka, whiskey, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake.
  2. Strain mixture into a glass and add cider and bitters; stir. Serve.
